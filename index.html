How It Works

Authenticate in 4 Simple Steps

From setup to consumer verification — AuthGuard is live in under 24 hours. No engineers required.

Register Your Brand

Create your account, set up your brand profile, and add your products in minutes — no technical setup needed.

Generate Secure QR Codes

Batch-generate encrypted, non-sequential QR codes. Download print-ready files to apply on packaging.

Ship & Distribute

Products ship with unique QR codes attached — each tied to batch, expiry, and product metadata in real time.

Consumer Verifies Instantly

Customers scan with any phone camera — no app needed — and get an instant genuine or suspect result.

Platform Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Encrypted QR Generation

Each code is cryptographically unique and non-sequential — duplication is practically impossible even with high-res scanning equipment.

No app, no friction. Consumers scan with their phone camera and get a clear authentic or suspect result in under 2 seconds.

Real-Time Scan Analytics

Live tracking of scans by city, region, device type, and timestamp — a full picture of where your products move and how.

Duplicate Scan Detection

Automatically flag codes scanned beyond the configured limit. Counterfeit hotspots become visible before they spread across your network.

Fraud Alerts & Notifications

Manufacturer Dashboard

Security Layer

Secure QR Codes That Are Practically Unclonable

AuthGuard QR codes are generated with AES-256 encryption and randomized UIDs — meaning no two codes are alike, and sequential duplication attacks fail completely.

  • Non-sequential, cryptographically signed UIDs per unit
  • Batch metadata: product name, batch ID, expiry date
  • Configurable max scan limits to block reuse
  • Print-ready PDF & ZIP exports, bulk download supported

Intelligence Layer

Analytics That Expose Counterfeit Hotspots

Every scan logs device, location, time, and IP. AuthGuard's engine surfaces anomaly patterns — so you know when a counterfeit ring is active before your customers do.

  • Real-time geo heat maps of scan activity by city
  • Total, unique, and duplicate scan counts per batch
  • Automated anomaly flagging with instant email alerts
  • CSV / Excel export for compliance and audit trails
